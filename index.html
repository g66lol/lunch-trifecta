Lunch Special Trifecta

All foods fall into one of three categories:

  • Soup
  • Salad
  • Sandwich

Or, two pseudo-categories:

  • Ingredient
  • Condiment

Some foods could arguably fit in two categories, so the best one has been chosen here.

For example, all open-face sandwiches could, arguably, be a salad.

Submit your items (or corrections) by submitting a pull request: https://github.com/g66lol/lunch-trifecta
